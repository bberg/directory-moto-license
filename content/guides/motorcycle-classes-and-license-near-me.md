# Motorcycle Classes & License Training Near Me

Searching for a motorcycle class near you is the first step toward your license. An approved rider course teaches the skills you need, supplies the training motorcycle and helmet, and in most states waives the DMV road test when you finish. Below are MSF-certified and state-approved programs you can use to find training in your area.

## Why Take a Local Motorcycle Class

- **Hands-on practice** on a provided motorcycle in a controlled range, not in traffic.
- **A road-test waiver** in most states, so you skip the DMV skills exam.
- **Insurance discounts** that many providers help you qualify for.
- **Confidence** built on the MSF Basic RiderCourse curriculum used nationwide.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I find a motorcycle class near me?
Pick your state above and follow the link to an approved provider, or [browse all listings](/) to filter by location and course type.

### Do motorcycle classes include the license test?
In most states, completing an approved course earns a DMV skills test waiver, so the course effectively replaces the road test. See our [test waiver guide](/guides/msf-basic-rider-course-test-waiver).

### Do I need my own motorcycle for the class?
Usually not. Most rider courses provide a training motorcycle and helmet for the riding portion. Confirm with the provider when you register.

### How much does a motorcycle class cost?
Pricing varies by provider and state. Many beginner courses run a few hundred dollars and include the road-test waiver, which can offset DMV fees.
